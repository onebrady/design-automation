Our Story

Founded in 1999 by industry veterans with decades of combined experience, Western Truck began as a small operation with just 5 trucks and a big vision: to revolutionize freight transportation through exceptional service and unwavering integrity.

What started as a local operation quickly grew into a regional powerhouse, and eventually a trusted national partner. Our commitment to safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

Today, we operate a modern fleet of over 200 trucks, serving clients across North America with comprehensive logistics solutions. But our core values remain unchanged: treating every shipment like it's our own, and every customer like family.
